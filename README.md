InterConnect – Internship & Entry-Level Job Portal

InterConnect is an intelligent internship and entry-level job recommendation platform designed to bridge the gap between student skills and industry opportunities. The platform analyzes a user’s technical skills, career interests, preferred job location, and resume to generate personalized job and internship recommendations.

The system goes beyond simple job listings by providing several career-focused tools that help users understand their readiness for the job market. It includes features such as Skill Gap Analysis, which identifies missing skills required for a chosen career path, and Resume & Confidence Scoring, which evaluates resumes and provides actionable feedback to improve them.

InterConnect also offers curated Learning Resources to help users acquire the skills needed for their target roles. For individuals considering a career transition, the Domain Switch Advisor provides guidance on shifting into new technical domains. Additionally, the Location Reality feature analyzes regional demand to help users understand job opportunities in their preferred locations.

By combining skill analysis, career guidance, resume evaluation, and personalized job recommendations, InterConnect aims to simplify the journey from learning to employment and empower students and fresh graduates to make informed career decisions.

Key Features

Personalized job and internship recommendations

Skill gap analysis for targeted career roles

Resume upload with feedback and confidence scoring

Curated learning resources to improve required skills

Domain switching guidance for career transitions

Location-based job demand insights

Clean and user-friendly interface for career exploration
